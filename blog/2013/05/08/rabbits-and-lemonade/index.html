Rabbits and Lemonade

May 8th, 2013

No Walrus.
No Queen of Hearts.
Not even the Mad Hatter.

Adventures disappeared from my mind…

Even still, I find a question that mattered:
Where are all of the walls in which I am confined
And what machine shall I build to ensure that they’re shattered?
