Books I'm Reading

May 1st, 2013

Here are some books that I’m reading right now, and they’re all fantastic.

  • Dune by Frank Herbert - this is a science fiction book involving intergalactic politics and complicated plots
  • Thinking Fast and Slow by Daniel Kahneman - this is a nonfiction book written by a Nobel Prize winner in economics about how we make sense of information and form conclusions
  • Titan by Ron Chernow - this is a biography of John D. Rockefeller, Sr. that is unfortunately dry at times, but has some great stories

I’m trying to get in the habit of reviewing books, so this is my way of holding myself accountable: I’ll post reviews by the end of May.
