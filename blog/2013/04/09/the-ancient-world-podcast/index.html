The Ancient World Podcast

Apr 9th, 2013

I just recently stubmled across The Ancient World, a podcast about ancient civilizations.

I’m beginning to really enjoy learning about history through audio, and this podcast brilliantly describes Ancient Mesopotamia really well, and it is still being updated with new episodes!

There are two (no longer updated) podcasts that are still worth listening to: Hardcore History and The History of Rome.

Learning about history can be fascinating for a simple (and trite) reason: past events often resemble our present predicaments.

One such example is the life of Lucius Cornelius Sulla, a general in Rome over 2,000 years ago.

Sulla was one of the first to break the rules of the constitution of the Roman Republic in an effort to preserve the values of the constitution.

Sound familiar?
